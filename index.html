About Us

Turner Painting & Construction

When Justin Turner was growing up he never thought painting would be his profession. He had long ago dreamed of purusing the medical field, but as opportunities arrived and his love for HGTV grew, he was drawn away from medicine and into the field of home remodeling. After working in the field for a few years and being shaped by his peers, he found himself desiring to step out on a dream and begin his own company and on July 10, 2015, Justin booked his first job when he was asked to paint three interior rooms of the Moon's household.
